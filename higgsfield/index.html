FAQ

Questions, answered

Do I need any video editing experience?

None at all. If you can describe a shot, you can direct one. The controls are designed for first-timers but deep enough for pros.

What can I create with Higgsfield?

Ads, music videos, short films, product reels, social content, mood boards, and animatics — anything that benefits from cinematic motion.

Who owns the videos I generate?

You do. On paid plans you get full commercial rights to everything you create.

How long does a render take?

Most clips render in 1–3 minutes. Paid plans get a priority queue for faster turnaround during peak hours.

Can my team collaborate?

Yes. Creator and Studio plans include shared workspaces, version history, and role-based access.
